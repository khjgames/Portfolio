I'm Kieran or KHJGames, a 22-year-old self-taught Canadian game developer.
I specialize in game design, programming, and GUI design.
At 6 years old, the first video game I played was pokemon silver version for the gameboy color.
Grew up playing GBC, GBA, DS, 3DS, PS1, PS2, PS3, Wii, GameCube, N64 and PC Games.
Made my first game when I was 12 years old with RPGMaker Vx Ace.
Main game engine experience -> Roblox Studio, Unity, GameMaker & RPGMaker VX Ace.
I've learned and programmed with Lua, Javascript, C, C++, C#, Java and Python.
I make graphics and GUIs in Paint.net. Also dabbled with aseprite, inkscape, krita, gimp & photoshop.
Dabbled in Davinci Resolve for video editing. Blender & Fusion 360 for 3D modeling.
I've been making games with Roblox Studio and lua for a decade.
My favourite projects are  Brick_Game,  Blades, and  Perseverance ~ Isekai MMORPG.
I hope to eventually fix them again and add footage to my portfolio.
